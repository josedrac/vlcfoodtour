Add your Snapchat Snapcode to your site. Get more followers!

== Description ==

The easy way to add your **Snapchat Snapcode** to any widget area on your site.  Simply download your [Snapcode](https://accounts.snapchat.com/accounts/snapcodes) and upload it to the widget, and you're ready to go!

**What's a Snapchat Snapcode?**

Snapchat introduced Snapcodes as an easy way to add friends on Snapchat. Simply point your Snapchat camera at a Snapcode and tap your screen to add!

This free Snapchat Widget for WordPress was developed by [pipdig](https://www.pipdig.co/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=wprepo&utm_campaign=snapchat). Feel free to tweet us [@pipdig](https://twitter.com/pipdig) if you have any questions, or post on the [support forum](https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/pipdig-snapcode-widget) if you need any help.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to 'Appearance > Widgets' in the dashboard and add the widget called "Snapchat Snapcode".
4. Download your Snapcode image file from [this link](https://accounts.snapchat.com/accounts/snapcodes?type=png).
5. Upload the image to the widget options in your WordPress dashboard.
